Kolgaon, Maharashtra

Kolgaon is a village in the Shrigonda Taluka of Ahmednagar district in Maharashtra state, India.





== Demographics ==

Covering 763 hectares (1,890 acres) and comprising 245 households at the time of the 2011 census of India, Kolgaon had a population of 999. There were 502 males and 497 females, with 94 people being aged six or younger.
