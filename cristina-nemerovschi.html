Cristina Nemerovschi

Cristina Nemerovschi (born May 10, 1980, in Bucharest) is a Romanian writer. She has received a lot of praise for her works and has been referred to as a "phenomenal writer", "the rebel of today's literature", and "a revelation" by Romanian literary magazines. She has published fifteen books so far. Nemerovschi received the TIUK (Talented Italians in UK) award for her debut novel, titled Book of the Year 2010, and the subsequent edition Book of the Year 2011, chosen from the finalists in the Premiers Romans En Lecture category. The author was also nominated for "Young Writers Gala - The best prose writer of 2014". Nemerovschi won a special prize from Athenaeum Magazine at the National Short Prose Contest Radu Rosetti.
