Anca Bergmann

Anca Bergmann (born (1976-02-21)21 February 1976) is a Romanian female former volleyball player, playing as a middle-blocker. She was part of the Romania women's national volleyball team.

She competed at the 2001 Women's European Volleyball Championship.
