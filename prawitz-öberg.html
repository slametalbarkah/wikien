Prawitz Öberg

Hartwig Edmund Prawitz Öberg (16 November 1930 – 4 November 1995) was a Swedish footballer and was named Swedish Football player of the year in 1962.

Born on 16 November 1930 in Gislöv, during his career he played defender, midfielder and forward. He got twenty-six caps and five goals for the Sweden national team. On club level he played for Malmö FF. He was awarded Guldbollen in 1962.

He later coached Lunds BK.

He died in Västra Skrävlinge on 4 November 1995 at the age of sixty-four.
