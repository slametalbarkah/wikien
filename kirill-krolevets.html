Kirill Krolevets

Kirill Yuryevich Krolevets (Russian: Кирилл Юрьевич Кролевец; born 7 October 1986) is a Russian former professional football player.





== Club career ==

He played two seasons in the Russian Football National League for FC Irtysh Omsk and FC Khimki.
