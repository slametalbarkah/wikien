Maksim Shilo

Maksim Shilo (Belarusian: Максім Шыла; Russian: Максим Шило; born 17 April 1993) is a Belarusian professional footballer who plays for Maxline.





== Career ==

During 2020–2021 he played for Dordoi Bishkek in Kyrgyzstan.
