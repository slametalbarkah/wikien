Sierzputy Młode

Sierzputy Młode [ɕɛʂˈputɨ ˈmwɔdɛ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Łomża, within Łomża County, Podlaskie Voivodeship, in north-eastern Poland. It lies approximately 7 kilometres (4 mi) west of Łomża and 80 km (50 mi) west of the regional capital Białystok.
