Anzhou, Mianyang

Anzhou District (simplified Chinese: 安州区; traditional Chinese: 安州區; pinyin: Ānzhōu Qū) formerly An County is a district of the city of Mianyang, in northeastern Sichuan province, China.

It has an area of 1,184 square kilometres (457 sq mi) and a population of 372,962 in 2020.





== Administrative divisions ==

Anzhou has 9 towns and 1 township.
