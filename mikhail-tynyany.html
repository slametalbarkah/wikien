Mikhail Tynyany

Mikhail Yuryevich Tynyany (Russian: Михаил Юрьевич Тыняный; born 22 May 1989) is a former Russian professional football player.





== Club career ==

He played 3 seasons in the Russian Football National League for FC KAMAZ Naberezhnye Chelny, FC Torpedo Moscow and FC Tambov.
